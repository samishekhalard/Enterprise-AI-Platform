# Architecture Board Charter

## Purpose

Govern architecture quality, compliance, and change decisions across EMS.

## Responsibilities

- Approve or reject major architecture changes.
- Review deviations/waivers and expiry.
- Ensure ADR and architecture documentation alignment.
- Monitor technical risk and architecture debt posture.

## Membership

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Chief/Lead Architect | Chair, final architecture decision accountability |
| Solution Architect | Cross-service design quality and implementation alignment |
| Security Representative | Security and compliance assurance |
| DevOps/SRE Representative | Operability, resilience, and deployment risk |
| Product Representative | Business value alignment |

## Decision Cadence

- Regular review: per sprint/release.
- Urgent review: on critical risk or blocking decision.

## Inputs

- ADR proposals/updates.
- TOGAF phase outputs.
- Architecture deviation requests.
- Risk and debt reports.
